Go on horseback into the mountains of Paraiso and visit the small farms of the community «Villa Nizao» accompanied by a local guide. You will spend time with the residents of the area, enjoying the scenery, lush nature and the local climate.

The tour starts early in the morning with the ascent to the mountains of Paraiso. The path leads past small communities up to Villa Nizao (small mountain village). During the ascent you will make a few stops where you can enjoy the panoramic view over the coast of Paraiso and the small farms in the area.

The way back leads through the fresh and clear water of the Nizaito river to the village of Paraiso. On the way there is another stop where you have the opportunity to visit a greenhouse where peppers are grown. Finally, you will return to the village where we will leave our horses and take the road back to your hotel by car.

Enjoy a horseback riding trip into the mountains of Paraiso. Visit small farms where bananas, coffee, cocoa and avocados are grown. Start your day heading to Paraiso, where the horses will be waiting for you.
